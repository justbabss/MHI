BREAKOUT CONFERENCE

It is a one day conference which holds every year in the month of June . it is a participatory program where teenagers are given opportunities to express their talents through presentations which includes: drama, special song rendition, recitation of poem and spoken word. This program is geared towards addressing key addressing key issues faced by teenagers.

PRISON VISITATION

MHI has made several visitations to two major prisons in Benin city Nigeria. i.e oko prison and Benin city prison During these verse visits, items are distributed to the inmates. These include, toiletries, clothes, slippers (foot wears), toothpastes and tooth brushes and food items.

MEDICAL OUTREACH

MHI has had several medical outreaches in Nigeria. and it includes health education and enlightment campaigns, free medical consultation, free drugs, free surgeries, free eye glasses and free laboratory tests. Our medical team comprise of several consultants, Snr. medical registrars, Snr. nurses, Snr. Lab scientist and technicians.

HOSPITAL VISITATION

MHI has also carried out several visitations to the hospitals. in the course of these visits, patients are given some basic items such as toiletries, toothpastes and brushes, slippers e.t.c. in some cases, patients who cannot pay their medical bills are given financial assistance by MHI.

PROVISION OF WATER AND ELECTRICITY

MHI has provided bore hole based water supply in some rural communities, these includes evbuotubu and umegbe communities. We also assisted the evbuotubu rural community with provision of electricty electricity poles for electrification of the community. in addition to this, we assisted with the annual maintenance of the intra community roads.
