Empowering the Needy: The Multidimensional Approach of Mercy House Initiative

Mercy House Initiative is a non-profit, non-governmental, faith-based organisation established in 1999 and dedicated to addressing these issues. We are on a mission to empower the needy and poor in society for development and a meaningful future.

Our approaches are multidimensional and geared at the transformation of the mind because we believe and have proven that many times and in different cultures that all things are possible to anyone who dares to believe and take corresponding actions.

We advance our agenda by working with young people, parents, community leaders, government agencies and policy makers to develop local capacity through seminars, workshops, conferences, medical outreaches, and numerous mentorship platforms which attract thousands of people yearly.

MHI we empower the poor and the needy in the society for development and meaningful future.
